Our Story

About Tonle Mekong

Founded in 2010, Tonle Mekong has been serving exquisite cuisine in a warm and elegant atmosphere. Our passion for culinary excellence drives us to create memorable dining experiences for our guests.

Led by award-winning Chef Michael Laurent, our kitchen team crafts each dish with precision and creativity, using only the freshest seasonal ingredients sourced from local farmers and suppliers.
